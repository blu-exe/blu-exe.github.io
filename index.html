About me

I'm just a high school student with my finger in all the pies because I want to taste all of them! I've collated them all here so that I can admire my own accomplishments and to build my self-esteem.

Music

I started piano from a pretty young age, and although I'm still pretty average at classical music, I've found myself liking jazz and composition much more because I love how freeing it is to both discover how I can use my knowledge from music theory and combine it with my musical skills to create something awesome.
